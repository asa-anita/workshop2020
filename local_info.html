Local information

Venue

The School and Workshop will take place at The School of Science, UNSW Canberra . Participants will require an Eduroam account if they want to have wireless internet access while on campus.



UNSW Canberra is approximately 6 minutes by car or 20 minutes by bike from the centre of Canberra, or approximately 30 minutes by bus.

Transport

From Canberra airport, the easiest way to access the conference venue is utilising a car or taxi/uber, which takes approximately 6 minutes. The conference venue is also accessible from the airport utilising public transport, via the route 5 then route 30 bus. This takes approximately 30 minutes.

Public transport in Canberra (bus and light rail along Northbourne Avenue) can be paid for utilsing cash fares or a MyWay card; however, the latter is recommended due to the lower fares, which are listed here. For adult passengers, a single MyWay trip costs $3.22, and the corresponding cash fare is $5.00. MyWay fares include a 90 minute transfer period, allowing you to take numerous bus/light rail services with no additional fare.

Workshop Dinner

There will be a workshop dinner as part of the conference on the Thursday, to be paid for by the attendee. Selection of the restaurant and subsequent pricing options will follow shortly.
